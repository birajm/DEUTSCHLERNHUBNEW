**Advanced Communication:**

* **Expressing Opinions (20 min):**

    * **Introduction:** Explain that expressing opinions is a crucial skill for effective communication in German. This lesson will cover various phrases for expressing agreement, disagreement, certainty, uncertainty, and qualifying opinions.

    * **Basic Phrases for Expressing Opinions:**

        * *Ich finde, dass...* (I think that...)

        * *Ich glaube, dass...* (I believe that...)

        * *Ich denke, dass...* (I think that...)

        * *Meiner Meinung nach...* (In my opinion...)

        * *Ich bin der Meinung, dass...* (I am of the opinion that...)

        * *Ich bin davon überzeugt, dass...* (I am convinced that...)

        * Provide example sentences for each phrase.

    * **Expressing Agreement:**

        * *Ich stimme dir/Ihnen zu.* (I agree with you.)

        * *Da stimme ich dir/Ihnen voll und ganz zu.* (I completely agree with you.)

        * *Das sehe ich genauso.* (I see it the same way.)

        * *Ich bin ganz deiner/Ihrer Meinung.* (I am completely of your opinion.)

        * *Das finde ich auch.* (I think so too.)

        * *Das ist richtig.* (That's right.)

        * Provide example sentences and emphasize the importance of providing reasons for agreement.

    * **Expressing Disagreement:**

        * *Ich stimme dir/Ihnen nicht zu.* (I don't agree with you.)

        * *Da bin ich anderer Meinung.* (I have a different opinion.)

        * *Das sehe ich anders.* (I see it differently.)

        * *Ich bin nicht deiner/Ihrer Meinung.* (I don't agree with you.)

        * *Das finde ich nicht.* (I don't think so.)

        * *Das ist falsch.* (That's wrong.)

        * Provide example sentences and emphasize the importance of providing reasons for disagreement and being polite.

    * **Expressing Certainty:**

        * *Ich bin sicher, dass...* (I am sure that...)

        * *Ich bin mir sicher, dass...* (I am certain that...)

        * *Ich weiß genau, dass...* (I know for sure that...)

        * *Ich bin fest davon überzeugt, dass...* (I am firmly convinced that...)

        * Provide example sentences.

    * **Expressing Uncertainty:**

        * *Ich bin mir nicht sicher, ob...* (I am not sure if...)

        * *Ich glaube nicht, dass...* (I don't think that...)

        * *Ich bin mir nicht ganz sicher, aber...* (I'm not entirely sure, but...)

        * *Es könnte sein, dass...* (It could be that...)

        * *Vielleicht...* (Maybe...)

        * Provide example sentences.

    * **Qualifying Opinions:**

        * *Einerseits..., andererseits...* (On the one hand..., on the other hand...)

        * *Zwar..., aber...* (It's true that..., but...)

        * *Ich verstehe zwar..., aber...* (I understand..., but...)

        * *Es stimmt, dass..., aber...* (It's true that..., but...)

        * Provide example sentences showing how to present different sides of an issue and reach a balanced conclusion.

    * **Practice Activities:**

        * Matching phrases to their meanings (agreement, disagreement, etc.).

        * Completing sentences with appropriate phrases.

        * Responding to given opinions with agreement or disagreement and providing reasons.

        * Formulating your own opinions on various topics.

        * Translating sentences that express opinions.

        * Role-playing scenarios where you have to express and discuss opinions.

        * Short writing exercises where you express your opinion on a given topic.
