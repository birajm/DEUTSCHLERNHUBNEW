Learning Levels

Our platform follows the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) for structuring content:

A1 Beginner

At this level, you'll learn basic phrases, simple grammar, and everyday vocabulary. You'll be able to introduce yourself, ask simple questions, and engage in basic conversations.

  • Alphabet and pronunciation
  • Basic greetings and introductions
  • Numbers and simple phrases
  • Present tense and basic articles

A2 Elementary

Building on A1, you'll expand your vocabulary and grammar knowledge. You'll learn to talk about your daily routine, make simple plans, and express basic opinions.

  • Everyday vocabulary (food, travel, shopping)
  • Past tense construction
  • Accusative case
  • Modal verbs

B1 Intermediate

At this level, you'll develop more fluency and be able to express yourself in most situations. You'll master more complex grammar and expand your vocabulary significantly.

  • Complex sentence structures
  • Dative and genitive cases
  • Subjunctive mood
  • Advanced conversation skills

Our Educational Approach

We believe in a balanced approach to language learning that includes:

Comprehensible Input

We provide learning materials slightly above your current level to challenge and expand your understanding.

Active Practice

Interactive exercises and quizzes help you apply what you've learned and reinforce your knowledge.

Spaced Repetition

Our system helps you review material at optimal intervals to improve long-term retention.

Real-World Context

We focus on practical situations and useful vocabulary that you'll encounter in everyday German conversations.
