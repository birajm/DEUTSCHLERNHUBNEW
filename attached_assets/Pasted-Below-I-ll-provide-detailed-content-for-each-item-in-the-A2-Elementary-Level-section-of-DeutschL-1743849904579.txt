### Expanded Basics

#### Small Talk (20 min)
- **Objective**: Learn how to make casual conversation in German.
- **Content**:
  - **Key Phrases**:
    - "Wie geht es dir heute?" – "How are you today?"
    - "Das Wetter ist schön, nicht wahr?" – "The weather is nice, isn’t it?"
    - "Was machst du am Wochenende?" – "What are you doing this weekend?"
  - **Structure**: Use present tense and simple questions.
  - **Tips**: Germans value directness; keep it short and friendly.
- **Example Dialogue**:
  - A: "Hallo, wie geht’s?" – "Hi, how are you?"
  - B: "Gut, danke! Und dir?" – "Good, thanks! And you?"
  - A: "Auch gut. Was machst du heute?" – "Also good. What are you doing today?"
- **Audio**: Record a native speaker saying these phrases (e.g., `small_talk.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Fill-in-the-blank: "___ geht es dir heute?" (Answer: Wie)

#### Asking for Information (25 min)
- **Objective**: Learn to ask for and understand detailed information.
- **Content**:
  - **Key Phrases**:
    - "Wo ist der Bahnhof?" – "Where is the train station?"
    - "Wann fährt der Bus?" – "When does the bus leave?"
    - "Wie viel kostet das?" – "How much does it cost?"
  - **Structure**: Use question words (wo, wann, wie) + verb in second position.
- **Example**:
  - "Kannst du mir sagen, wo die Toilette ist?" – "Can you tell me where the bathroom is?"
- **Audio**: Record questions and responses (e.g., `asking_info.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Match question to answer: "Wann kommt der Zug?" → "Um 14 Uhr."

#### Expressing Opinions (20 min)
- **Objective**: Learn to express likes, dislikes, and opinions.
- **Content**:
  - **Key Phrases**:
    - "Ich mag Kaffee." – "I like coffee."
    - "Ich finde das nicht so gut." – "I don’t think that’s so great."
    - "Meiner Meinung nach ist das toll." – "In my opinion, that’s great."
  - **Structure**: Use "mag" (like), "finde" (find/think), or "meiner Meinung nach" (in my opinion).
- **Example**:
  - "Ich mag Pizza, aber ich finde Pasta besser." – "I like pizza, but I think pasta is better."
- **Audio**: Record opinions (e.g., `opinions.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Translate: "I don’t like rain." (Answer: "Ich mag Regen nicht.")

#### Telling Stories (25 min)
- **Objective**: Learn to tell simple stories in German.
- **Content**:
  - **Key Phrases**:
    - "Gestern war ich im Park." – "Yesterday I was in the park."
    - "Dann habe ich ein Buch gelesen." – "Then I read a book."
    - "Es war sehr schön." – "It was very nice."
  - **Structure**: Use past tense (Perfekt) and connectors (dann, und).
- **Example**:
  - "Am Samstag habe ich meine Freunde getroffen. Wir haben Kaffee getrunken." – "On Saturday, I met my friends. We drank coffee."
- **Audio**: Record a short story (e.g., `story.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Order sentences: "Ich habe gegessen. Ich war hungrig. Dann habe ich geschlafen."

### Grammar

#### Past Tense (Perfekt) (30 min)
- **Objective**: Learn the present perfect tense in German.
- **Content**:
  - **Rule**: "haben" or "sein" (auxiliary) + past participle (e.g., gemacht, gegangen).
  - **Examples**:
    - "Ich habe ein Buch gelesen." – "I have read a book." (haben + gelesen)
    - "Ich bin nach Hause gegangen." – "I have gone home." (sein + gegangen)
  - **Tips**: Most verbs use "haben"; motion verbs (gehen, kommen) use "sein."
- **Audio**: Record examples (e.g., `perfekt.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Conjugate: "Ich ___ (spielen) Fußball." (Answer: "Ich habe Fußball gespielt.")

#### Modal Verbs (25 min)
- **Objective**: Learn modal verbs (können, müssen, wollen, etc.).
- **Content**:
  - **Key Verbs**:
    - "können" – "can": "Ich kann schwimmen." – "I can swim."
    - "müssen" – "must": "Ich muss arbeiten." – "I must work."
    - "wollen" – "want": "Ich will essen." – "I want to eat."
  - **Structure**: Modal verb in second position, infinitive at the end.
- **Example**:
  - "Ich will morgen ins Kino gehen." – "I want to go to the cinema tomorrow."
- **Audio**: Record sentences (e.g., `modal_verbs.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Fill-in: "Ich ___ (müssen) lernen." (Answer: "Ich muss lernen.")

#### Prepositions (30 min)
- **Objective**: Learn German prepositions and their cases.
- **Content**:
  - **Accusative**: "durch," "für," "gegen," "ohne," "um."
    - "Ich gehe durch den Park." – "I go through the park."
  - **Dative**: "aus," "bei," "mit," "nach," "von," "zu."
    - "Ich fahre mit dem Bus." – "I ride with the bus."
  - **Tips**: Memorize case-specific prepositions.
- **Audio**: Record examples (e.g., `prepositions.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Choose: "Ich gehe ___ (in/zu) die Schule." (Answer: "Ich gehe zur Schule.")

#### Conjunctions (25 min)
- **Objective**: Learn to connect sentences in German.
- **Content**:
  - **Coordinating**: "und" (and), "aber" (but), "oder" (or).
    - "Ich mag Tee, aber ich trinke Kaffee." – "I like tea, but I drink coffee."
  - **Subordinating**: "weil" (because), "dass" (that).
    - "Ich lerne, weil ich besser sprechen will." – "I learn because I want to speak better."
  - **Tips**: Subordinating conjunctions send the verb to the end.
- **Audio**: Record sentences (e.g., `conjunctions.mp3`).
- **Practice**: Combine: "Ich bin müde. Ich habe viel gearbeitet." (Answer: "Ich bin müde, weil ich viel gearbeitet habe.")
