Tips for Effective Reading Practice
Start with What You Know

Begin with topics you're familiar with to make comprehension easier.

Don't Look Up Every Word

Try to understand the context first, then only look up key words you don't know.

Read Aloud

Reading aloud improves pronunciation and helps you connect written and spoken German.

Regular Practice

Even just 10 minutes of reading per day can significantly improve your German skills.

Use Context Clues

Try to guess the meaning of unfamiliar words from the context before checking a dictionary.

Review and Re-read

Reading the same text multiple times helps reinforce vocabulary and grammar structures.
